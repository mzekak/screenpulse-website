Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Screen Pulse, we believe your personal information belongs to you. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our applications.

Information We Collect

We are committed to collecting only the minimum information necessary to provide our services:

  • Local Data: Most of our apps store your data locally on your device. This includes your preferences, settings, and any content you create within the app.
  • Usage Information: We may collect anonymous analytics data to improve app performance and user experience. This data is aggregated and cannot identify individual users.
  • Device Information: Basic device information (device type, operating system version) may be collected for compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for:

  • Providing and improving our app functionality
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Understanding how users interact with our apps to enhance user experience
  • Responding to support requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your data security is paramount:

  • Data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • We use industry-standard encryption for any data transmission
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

Third-Party Services

Some of our apps may use third-party services (such as analytics providers). These services operate under their own privacy policies. We carefully select partners who share our commitment to user privacy.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics collection
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@screenpulse.shop

Ibadah Focus - Specific Privacy Information

Data Storage: All prayer times, habit tracking, and personal settings are stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this information.

No Account Required: Ibadah Focus does not require account creation or user registration.

Offline First: The app works completely offline. No internet connection is required for core functionality.

Notifications: Local notifications are used to remind you of prayer times and habits. These are managed entirely on your device.

Ru'ya - Specific Privacy Information

Dream Data Storage: All dream entries and interpretations are stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to your dream content or personal interpretations.

AI Processing: When you request dream interpretation, only the dream description you choose to submit is sent to our AI service for analysis. No personal information is transmitted.

No Account Required: Ru'ya does not require account creation or user registration to use core features.

Dream Journal Privacy: Your dream journal is completely private and stored locally on your device. We cannot access, read, or share your dreams.

Analytics: We may collect anonymized usage data (e.g., number of interpretations requested) to improve the app, but this data cannot be linked to individual users or specific dream content.

Educational Content: References to Islamic scholars and traditional interpretations are for educational purposes. We do not track which interpretations you view.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through our apps or via email. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
