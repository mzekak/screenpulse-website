Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Screen Pulse, we believe your personal information belongs to you. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our applications.

Information We Collect

We are committed to collecting only the minimum information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Some apps may require you to create an account using your email address. This allows features like syncing data across devices and account recovery. Email addresses are stored securely and used exclusively for authentication and account management.
  • User Content: Content you create within our apps (such as journal entries, settings, or input for app features) may be stored locally on your device or, if you create an account, securely in the cloud to enable cross-device synchronization.
  • Usage Information: We may collect anonymous analytics data to improve app performance and user experience. This data is aggregated and cannot identify individual users.
  • Device Information: Basic device information (device type, operating system version) may be collected for compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for:

  • Providing and improving our app functionality
  • User authentication and account management
  • Syncing user data across devices (when accounts are used)
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Understanding how users interact with our apps to enhance user experience
  • Responding to support requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your data security is paramount:

  • Data may be stored locally on your device or securely in the cloud (when using account features)
  • We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmission and storage
  • Account data is stored through secure, reputable third-party infrastructure providers (such as Supabase) that comply with industry security standards
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information
  • We regularly review and update our security practices
  • Email addresses and passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text

Third-Party Services

Some of our apps use carefully selected third-party services to provide functionality:

  • Authentication Services: We use Supabase for secure user authentication and account management. Supabase adheres to strict security and privacy standards.
  • AI Services: Some apps use AI services to provide features like interpretation or analysis. Only the specific content you choose to submit is sent to these services.
  • Analytics: We may use analytics providers to understand app usage patterns. These analytics are anonymized and aggregated.

These services operate under their own privacy policies. We carefully select partners who share our commitment to user privacy and do not share your data with partners for advertising or marketing purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data and account
  • Opt-out of analytics collection (where available in app settings)
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Update or correct your account information
  • Use our apps without creating an account (where offline functionality is available)

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@screenpulse.shop

Ibadah Focus - Specific Privacy Information

Data Storage: All prayer times, habit tracking, and personal settings are stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this information.

No Account Required: Ibadah Focus does not require account creation or user registration.

Offline First: The app works completely offline. No internet connection is required for core functionality.

Notifications: Local notifications are used to remind you of prayer times and habits. These are managed entirely on your device.

Ru'ya - Specific Privacy Information

Account Creation: Users can optionally create an account using their email address to sync their dream journal across devices and access their interpretations from multiple devices. Email addresses are stored securely through our authentication provider (Supabase) and are used exclusively for authentication, account recovery, and account management. We never share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

Dream Data Storage: Dream entries are stored securely in your account when you're signed in, allowing you to access them across devices. All data is encrypted and protected. Users who choose not to create an account can still use the app with local-only storage.

Data We Collect: When you create an account, we collect your email address and assign a unique user ID. When you request dream interpretations, we process your dream descriptions through our AI service to provide interpretations.

AI Processing: When you request dream interpretation, only the dream description you choose to submit is sent to our AI service for analysis. Your email address and other personal information are not included in AI requests.

Dream Journal Privacy: Your dream journal is private and accessible only to you. We cannot access, read, or share your dreams with others. Your dreams are encrypted during transmission and storage.

Analytics: We may collect anonymized usage data (e.g., number of interpretations requested, features used) to improve the app. This analytics data is not linked to your email address or personal identity and cannot identify individual users or specific dream content.

Educational Content: References to Islamic scholars and traditional interpretations are for educational purposes. We do not track which interpretations you view or link this information to your account.

No Tracking or Advertising: We do not use your data for advertising purposes, do not share data with advertising networks or data brokers, and do not track you across other apps or websites.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through our apps or via email. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
